# NursingService
As members of the university community, some of the problems currently facing 
the ENES nursing service are known from our own experience. 
The main problem was identified as the lack of organization in the management of waiting times between appointments,
as well as the absence of a 
system by which it can be known when your appointment will be attended, the approximate waiting time, 
and the times or slots that are already occupied by other activities within the nursing area.
